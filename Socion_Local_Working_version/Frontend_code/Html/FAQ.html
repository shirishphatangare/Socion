Frequently asked questions about Socion

1) How do I register to oursocion.com?

You can register to Socion on website www.oursocion.com by following link 'Join Socion'.

2) How can I retrieve my password in socion?

You can retrieve your password by following 'Forgot your Password' link on www.oursocion.com. Enter your username or email-id and your password will be sent to your registered email-id

3) OK, I am registered with Socion, but my inbox is empty, how to proceed?

Your inbox is empty because you are new to the system and do not have enough buddies. Your buddy count is zero currently. You can see buddy list by following link 'Socion Buddies'. You can add people to your buddy list by searching them by their name and/or surname in 'search people' feature on 'Socion Buddies' page. There is another way to add buddies if you do not know names of registered people. You can see stories on 'Browse Stories' page and rate them. 'Browse stories' page gives you opportunity to browse recent stories posted by registered members and develope new relationships through rating their stories. When you have added some buddies, chances of getting stories in your inbox will increase.

4) What is 'Change Inbox Settings' link on 'Inbox' page?

This link will take you to the 'Change Inbox Settings' page wherein you can personalise settings for your inbox.

a) Effect of Story Age: Here you can set whether you want old or recent stories in your inbox. If you set this option to 'Low' that means you would receive older stories along with recent stories. However, if you set it to 'High' means you would receive only recently posted stories and not older stories in your inbox.

b) Inbox Story Categories: With this option, you can choose to receive stories in your Inbox, only from selected categories.

c) No. of Inbox items: You can set how many maximum items you want in your inbox.

d) Show Story since Last X Days: You can set option to filter out stories older than last X days.

6) How can I post a story in Socion?

You can post story on 'Submit story' screen. On this screen you need to enter title for your story followed by online link for it and the select category from availbale categories on this page. Once all above options are entered you can click 'Submit story' link to submit story to Socion.

7) What is difference between Favourite Stories and Top Stories?

'Favourite Stories' page shows your favourite stories, that is stories you have rated to five stars.

'Top Stories' page shows current top 10 stories in the system.

8) What is 'Browse Stories' page?

'Browse Stories' page allows you to browse stories in the system. You can browse stories categorically on this page. You can also search stories on the basis of its title.

9) Can I manually add/remove buddies?

Yes, it can be done on 'Socion Buddies' page. It shows list of your current socion buddies. You can also search and add other socion registered users.

10) Which browsers are recommended ?

For current version we recommend Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome and Safari.
