What is Socion?

Socion is a social news aggregator. Online content is distributed throughout the web in the form of videos, blogs, pictures, E-books, News and so on.. Socion provides a way to share and receive online content. Socion has a concept of personalised inbox, wherein user can configure inbox algorithm. With Socion, registered user can connect to other Socion users (buddies) and share stories with them. Users develop trust relationships with other users, which are updated dynamically through voting. Spammers can be kept away from inbox by repeatedly voting them down. Socion facilitates bookmarking of favourite links.

Innovative concepts in Socion:


A) Personalised inbox:

Your personalised inbox can be configured on Change Inbox Settings screen shown above. Effect of news age indicates news age decay coefficient. There are three options: Low, Medium and High. Effect of story age on inbox increases from low to high. Lower setting means, inbox receives older stories and higher setting means, inbox receives more recent stories.

B) Concept of trust:

Trust concept in Socion is dynamic, that means trust relationships are updated as and when users vote stories posted by each other. Voting is done on the scale of 0.5 to 5.0. Voting helps likely minded users to form close relationships with each other. Voting also facilitates to keep spammers away from inbox. On Socion buddies screen, user can see list of other users currently close to it (buddies). User can also manually search and add other registered users as buddies.

C) Bookmarking favourite stories:

In Socion, one can bookmark his favourite stories for future reference. The stories for which user rates to five stars are considered as his favourite stories and are saved in the database.

D) Top stories:

In Socion, one can view top stories by category. Top stories are ranked with an algorithm which takes into account factors like story age, ratings by other users and number of votes.

E) Browse recent stories:

Latest stories posted by socion registered users can be viewed on the browse stories page. Stories can be browsed by category. This view gives user a chance to develop new trust relationships with other registered users.

F) Postbox:

Postbox for user shows all the stories posted in the system by the registered user.
